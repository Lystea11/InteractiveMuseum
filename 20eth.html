Relevancy of the British Monarchy Today:

As we have talked about the cons of a Monarchy government, having a head or kingpin of a nation could be very useful for things like keeping thousand-year-old traditions, having shared Values and Principles with the people and a reminder of their cultural and historical heritage. Monarchies in the present day also exercise soft power and diplomacy, strengthening relationships between commonwealth countries which is important to keep the natural dignity of the crown.

Conclusion

A Monarchist government has been present through many centuries in history and with many different countries but never like the way we see in the British Empire and present-day Great Britain. Though Monarchies are a thing of the past, having shared values and principles, keeping traditions and overall strengthening diplomacy can be reasons why a light Monarchy could be useful and how it made sense to make a change back in the feudal origins of the British Empire.
